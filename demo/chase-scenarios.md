# Where in the World is Chargers, San Diego?
## "5-Question Escalating Chase" — 10 Scenario Drafts

**Format:** Each round = 4 "henchmen" questions (geographic answers) that share a hidden thread.
The thread is the deduction that reveals the round's mystery boss ("Carmen") in Q5.
Player drops a pin on a globe each question; scored 0–5000 by great-circle distance.
Difficulty escalates Q1→Q5 (vaguer clues, tighter pin tolerance, faster clock).

**Thread-type legend** (how the 4 henchmen connect to the boss):
- 🔗 **TEAMMATES** — all played together / same roster
- 🏟️ **SAME PLACE** — all tied to one city/stadium/college
- 🏅 **SAME EVENT** — same draft class, Super Bowl, Olympics, game
- 🎓 **SAME SCHOOL** — college pipeline
- 🧬 **CATEGORY** — looser: all share a trait the boss epitomizes
- 🌍 **GEO-PATTERN** — the pin locations themselves spell the answer (spiciest)

## What the 10 drafts taught us (complexity & value read)

**Thread types ranked by author-effort vs. payoff:**

| Thread type | Authoring effort | "Aha" payoff | Scenarios it can yield |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🏟️ Same place (all → one stadium/city) | Low | Medium | ~Unlimited (every franchise) |
| 🏅 Same event (draft/SB/Olympics) | Low–Med | High | High (decades of drafts, 60 SBs) |
| 🔗 Teammates (one roster/era) | Medium | **Highest** | High (every dynasty) |
| 🎓 Same school (college pipeline) | Medium | High | Medium (handful of factory programs) |
| 🧬 Category (loose trait) | **Lowest** | Lowest | Unlimited but forgettable |
| 🌍 Geo-cipher (letters spell answer) | **Highest** | Very high | Low — boss rounds only |

**Key findings:**
1. **The thread does NOT need to be tight to be fun** — but the tighter it is, the bigger the Q5 payoff. Teammates & same-event are the sweet spot.
2. **Don't repeat a city inside one round** (Scenarios 4, 5 leaked) — clusters the pins and tips the answer.
3. **The boss should require a leap, not a lookup** — Q5 works when the 4 henchmen *imply* the boss without naming the relationship. Worst when Q5 is just a 5th trivia question (Scenario 2 muddied this).
4. **Mix difficulty across the 5** — pure-geography rounds (Scenario 8) make great openers/tutorials; geo-cipher (9) makes a great finale.
5. **Cross-sport (Olympics, two-sport stars) is the pressure valve** — it multiplies the scenario pool so we don't exhaust football trivia.

**Recommended starter pack for the build:** Scenarios **1 (Patriots), 3 (Greatest Show), 6 (Lambeau), 7 (Olympic crossover), 8 (Super Bowl road trip)** — covers all the satisfying thread types, spreads the globe geographically, and escalates cleanly from easy (8) to clever (1).
